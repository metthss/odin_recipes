Pasta alle Vongole

Description

Pasta alle Vongole is a very good and yummy dish that I enjoy cooking and eating whenever I can. It is so damn good!

Italians prepare this dish two ways: in bianco, i.e., with oil, garlic, parsley, and sometimes a splash of white wine; and in rosso, like the former but with tomatoes and fresh basil, the addition of tomatoes being more frequent in the south. Traditionally, the bivalves are cooked quickly in hot olive oil to which plenty of garlic has been added. The live clams open during cooking, releasing a liquid that serves as the primary flavoring agent.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Sale
  • Pepe
  • Olio
  • Aglio
  • Peperoncino
  • Prezzemolo
  • Alici
  • Vino Bianco
  • Vongole

Red Version

Add the following ingredients to the previous list:

  • Pomodori

Steps

  1. Add some Olio to a pan. Cook the Aglio, then add Peperoncino, Sale and Pepe
  2. Meanwhile, boil some water in a pot: we will use it to cook the Spaghetti
  3. Add Vongole to the pan, together with a sprinkle of Vino Bianco
  4. When the Pasta is cooked, add some water from the pot where you cooked the Pasta to the pan. Add the Pasta to the pan and cook everything together for a couple minutes
  5. Enjoy!
